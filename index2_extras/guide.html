User Guide

Welcome to Khet Sahayak! Follow these simple steps to get started and make the most of our platform.

Create an Account

Sign up with your email and verify your profile to get started with Khet Sahayak.

Explore Rental/Listing

Access the rental marketplace to find or offer farm equipment or land for your agricultural needs.

Use Crop Recommendation

Input details about your farm's soil, location, and preferences to receive tailored crop options.

Fertilizer Guidance

Receive fertilizer type and dosage recommendations suited to your crop and soil conditions.

Disease Identification

Upload clear photos of affected plants to view identified diseases and receive treatment tips.

Support

Use the contact/support section for questions or assistance. Our team is here to help you.

Quick Tips

  • Keep your profile updated for better recommendations
  • Use high-quality images for disease identification
  • Regularly check the marketplace for new equipment listings
  • Consult with local experts alongside our recommendations
  • Enable notifications to stay updated on rental requests

If you face any difficulties please feel free to contact our support team.
